About Chittoor

Chittoor is a city and district headquarters in Chittoor district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.

It is also the mandal and divisional headquarters of Chittoor mandal and Chittoor revenue division respectively.

city has a population of 153,756 and that of the agglomeration is 175,647.
